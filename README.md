# 🛒 Supermarket Sales Dashboard

A beginner-friendly Power BI dashboard built using supermarket sales data to analyze business performance, customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, and sales trends.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides an interactive overview of supermarket operations through key performance indicators and visual analytics.

Users can filter data using:

- City
- Product Line

The dashboard updates dynamically based on selected filters, enabling deeper analysis of sales performance across different segments.

---

## 🚀 Features

### KPI Metrics
- 💰 Total Sales
- 📈 Total Revenue
- 📦 Total Units Sold
- ⭐ Average Customer Rating

### Visualizations
- Product Category Sales Analysis
- City-wise Sales Comparison
- Payment Method Distribution
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Interactive Slicers

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Food & Beverages is the Top Performing Category
- Generated approximately **56K** in sales.
- Highest selling product category among all segments.

### 2️⃣ Naypyitaw Generates the Highest Sales
- Recorded approximately **111K** in sales.
- Slightly outperformed Yangon and Mandalay.

### 3️⃣ Sales Are Well Distributed Across Cities
| City | Sales |
|--------|--------|
| Naypyitaw | 111K |
| Yangon | 106K |
| Mandalay | 106K |

This indicates balanced business performance across branches.

### 4️⃣ Cash is the Most Preferred Payment Method
- Contributes approximately **34.74%** of total sales.
- Closely followed by E-wallet and Credit Card payments.

### 5️⃣ Strong Sales Performance in Q1
- January, February, and March generated the highest sales.
- January alone contributed approximately **87K** in sales.

### 6️⃣ Sales Declined After March
- Significant drop observed from April onwards.
- Indicates potential seasonality or changing customer demand.

### 7️⃣ Positive Customer Satisfaction
- Average customer rating: **6.97 / 10**
- Reflects generally satisfactory customer experience.
